About Me

I'm Md. Masrafi Bin Seraj, hailing from the historic district Bagerhat, Bangladesh. Currently, I'm in my final year pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Jashore University of Science and Technology (JUST). Computers and coding have always fascinated me, serving as the ultimate playground for solving intricate puzzles and unraveling the wonders of technology.

Throughout my academic journey, I've enthusiastically participated in a variety of programming contests, both online and offline. Competing in renowned events such as the ICPC Regionals and various IUPCs has not only been a thrilling experience but has also been instrumental in refining my coding skills and fostering a spirit of continuous improvement.

Beyond competitions, my passion extends to full-stack development, where I revel in the process of crafting seamless digital experiences from front-end design to back-end functionality. Additionally, my curiosity is piqued by the vast potential of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, as I explore the exciting realm of teaching machines to emulate human cognition.
